What is Spin to Win?

Spin to Win, an engaging segment of ShareTrip app which has been rapidly popular to the users after launching the country’s first travel app “ShareTrip” series of months back. Spin to win is all about giving a try in favor of your luck with a little fun.

Below are some basic spin to win rules or regulations, and how to play the game, and game summary.

Game Summary and How to Play

Spin to win is a virtual wheel which you will find on the ShareTrip app and website homepage as a special feature. It offers you exciting gifts and trip coins which you can avail or redeem later. Gifts and trip coins are placed on the wheel which you can see. What you have to do is, just tap on spin and see what luck offers you.

In the ShareTrip app, each coin contains the value of BDT 1. After installing the app in your device, your available coin will be shown on the right top of your screen. To spin the wheel, each try will cost you 50 trip coins which will be deducted from your available trip coins from your app profile. Every spin requires 50 trip coins minimum. If your coin turns zero, no worries. You will be offered 50 trip coins after every 6 hours, just tap on the treasure box written “free coins” which is just beside spin to win icon. There is a leaderboard from where you can see the top ranked 10 users in terms of trip coins.

By playing Spin to Win, users can avail exciting gifts or trip coins from our app. Gifts won by the users have specific validity, users have to claim it as per our T&C. Users can also redeem their trip coin to avail any service that exists in the app.
